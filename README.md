# 📊 Project 4 – E-Commerce Performance Dashboard

> DecodeLabs Industrial Training Kit – Batch 2026

## Overview

This project focuses on transforming raw e-commerce data into an interactive business dashboard that helps stakeholders understand sales performance, customer behavior, and business trends.

Using the same 1,200-order dataset from the previous projects, the data was cleaned, analyzed, and visualized to create a professional dashboard that supports business decision-making.

The dashboard summarizes key performance indicators (KPIs), highlights important trends, and provides actionable recommendations based on data.

---

## Project Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Transform raw business data into meaningful visual insights.
- Present complex information in a simple and interactive format.
- Identify business trends and performance indicators.
- Support data-driven decision making through visualization.

The analysis is based on an e-commerce dataset containing **1,200 customer orders** collected between **January 2023 and June 2025**.

## Key Insights

### Revenue Performance

- Total Revenue exceeded **$1.26 Million**.
- A total of **1,200 orders** were analyzed.
- The average order value was approximately **$1,054**.

---

### Order Status

Nearly **41.4%** of all orders were either cancelled or returned.

This indicates that improving the fulfillment process should be a business priority before increasing marketing efforts.

---

### Revenue Trend

Revenue showed a gradual decline during the analysis period, suggesting the need to improve customer retention and operational efficiency.

---

### Product Performance

Chair and Printer generated the highest revenue among all products.

Together, these products contributed approximately **31%** of total revenue.

---

### Marketing Performance

Instagram generated the highest revenue and the largest number of customer orders, making it the most effective marketing channel.

---

### Payment Methods

Revenue was evenly distributed across all payment methods.

No single payment option dominated customer preferences.

---

### Coupon Analysis

Orders placed without coupons generated revenue similar to coupon-based orders.

This suggests that coupons currently have limited influence on increasing average order value.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

- Reduce cancellations and returns by improving the checkout and fulfillment process.
- Prioritize inventory and marketing for high-performing products such as Chair and Printer.
- Increase investment in Instagram marketing campaigns.
- Review coupon strategies to determine whether discounts are improving sales.
- Continue supporting multiple payment methods since customer usage is well balanced.
